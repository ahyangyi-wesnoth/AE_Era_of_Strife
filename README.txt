Era of Strife (Ageless Edition)
===============================

The original [Era of Strife](https://forums.wesnoth.org/viewtopic.php?t=21136) was unmaintained after Wesnoth 1.10. However, its factions were included and maintained in Ageless Era. This add-on simply re-creates the Era of Strife by using material from Ageless Era.

Maintainer of this mod: Ahyangyi

Of course, this is just a simple wrapper and actual credits go to the original authors of Era of Strife and maintainers of Ageless Era.
